Marc-André ter Stegen (German pronunciation: [ˌmaɐ̯k ʔanˈdʁeː teːɐ̯ ˈsteːɡn̩; - ʔãˈd-];[3] born 30 April 1992) is a German professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for the Spanish club Barcelona and the Germany national team.[4][5][6] After four seasons in the Bundesliga with Borussia Mönchengladbach, making 108 league appearances, he joined Barcelona for €12 million in 2014. He won the treble in his first season in Spain, playing for Barcelona in Copa del Rey and UEFA Champions League.
